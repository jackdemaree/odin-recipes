BLT Sandwhich

Description

A BLT is a type of sandwhich, named for the initials of its primary ingredients, bacon, lettuce, and tomato. It can be made with varying recipes according to personal preference. Simple variants include using different types of lettuce, toasting or not, or adding mayonnaise.

Ingredients
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 strips of bacon
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Turkey
  • Mayonnaise
Steps
  1. Toast 2 slices of bread
  2. Place 1 slice of bread on plate
  3. Place turkey on top of bread
  4. Place bacon on top of turkey
  5. Place lettuce on top of bacon
  6. Place mayonnaise on top of lettuce
  7. Place 2nd slice of bread on top and slice to how you enjoy
