Taco Salad

Description

Recipe that combines beef, lettuce, tomatoes and chips in a delicious blend that will satisfy your tastebuds and fill your stomach

Ingredients
  • Beef
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Chips
Steps
  1. Cook Beef
  2. Chop Lettuce
  3. Chop Tomatoes
  4. Place Beef, Lettuce, Tomatoes and chips in bowl
  5. Mix
